Welcome to AtoB

What does AtoB do?

Since early stages of English education, grammar and vocabulary always prioritised and practising stayed in the background. In order to solve this problem we provide reading chapters so that you can read them and get a score based on your performance. This way you can find placed you are having trouble with and improve yourself. 

How do we create content

Team of selected teachers write our reading chapters considering metrics like difficulty, intriguing topic and many more
